<section class="section">
    <h2>Selected Artifacts</h2>

    <div class="resume-entry">
        <h3>PHIL 355E – Zero Days Case Study</h3>
        <p class="resume-meta">Cybersecurity Ethics</p>

        <p>
            This case study applied rule utilitarianism to evaluate the ethical implications of 
            publicly disclosing information about the Stuxnet cyberweapon. The analysis considered 
            competing responsibilities between protecting public safety, preventing future attacks, 
            and minimizing geopolitical consequences.
        </p>

        <p>
            By evaluating multiple ethical considerations, I concluded that responsible
            disclosure provides greater long-term societal benefit through transparency,
            accountability, and informed security practices.
        </p>

    <div class="resume-entry">
        <h3>CYSE 406 – Cyber Law Writing Assignment</h3>
        <p class="resume-meta">Cyber Law</p>

        <p>
            This assignment examined the balance between privacy, security, and freedom
            of expression within modern digital communication platforms. I evaluated
            services including Discord and Microsoft Teams while analyzing moderation
            practices, user privacy, and data protection responsibilities.
        </p>

        <p>
            The paper also explored how organizations manage sensitive personal
            information and discussed legal and societal implications resulting from
            cybersecurity incidents and data breaches.
        </p>

        <a class="button" href="{{ '/assets/documents/writing-research/ethics-law-policy/James Davis - Cyber Law 406 Writing Assignment Spring 2025.pdf' | relative_url }}">View Artifact</a>
    </div>

    <div class="resume-entry">
        <h3>CYSE 425W – NIST Cybersecurity Framework Policy Analysis</h3>
        <p class="resume-meta">Cybersecurity Strategy &amp; Policy</p>

        <p>
            This policy analysis evaluated the effectiveness of the NIST Cybersecurity
            Framework through organizational, ethical, and governance perspectives. I
            examined how the framework supports risk management while identifying
            challenges related to implementation consistency, accountability, and
            organizational maturity.
        </p>

        <p>
            The project proposed recommendations for improving framework evaluation
            through stakeholder feedback and cybersecurity maturity models, emphasizing
            continuous improvement and practical adoption.
        </p>
